Resource Profile: PGHDCommunication

Official URL: https://w3id.org/pghdprovo/fhir/StructureDefinition/pghd-communication Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-09-21 Computable Name: PGHDCommunication

Captures communication events where patient generated health data (PGHD) is shared, discussed, or clarified between patients, caregivers, practitioners, or organizations.
