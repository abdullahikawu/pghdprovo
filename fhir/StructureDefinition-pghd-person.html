Resource Profile: PGHDPerson

Official URL: https://w3id.org/pghdprovo/fhir/StructureDefinition/pghd-person Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-09-21 Computable Name: PGHDPerson

Details about a person who may be linked to Patient Generated Health Data, such as a caregiver or related individual. Represents a non-patient individual connected to PGHD, with identifiers, contact details, and links to patient records to ensure accurate linkage to clinical systems.
