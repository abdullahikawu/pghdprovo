Resource Profile: PGHDPractitioner

Official URL: https://w3id.org/pghdprovo/fhir/StructureDefinition/pghd-practitioner Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-09-21 Computable Name: PGHDPractitioner

Details about a doctor or health worker who checks or uses patient health data and/or patient generated health data (PGHD). Represents a healthcare practitioner who validates or works with patient health data, with required details like name and ID.

Usages:

  • Refer to this Profile: PGHDCommunication, PGHDEncounter and PGHDProvenance
  • Examples for this Profile: Practitioner/PractitionerExample
