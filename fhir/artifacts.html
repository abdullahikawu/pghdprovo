This page provides a list of the FHIR artifacts defined as part of this implementation guide.

Structures: Resource Profiles

These define constraints on FHIR resources for systems conforming to this implementation guide.

PGHDDevice

Describes a tool, like a health tracking devices or Blood Presure Monitor, used to collect patient health data. Details about a device, such as a wearable, that collects patient health data, ensuring that the information about the data source is provided.
PGHDEncounter

Records a session or visit where patient health data and/or patient generated health data (PGHD) is collected or reviewed. Captures details of a healthcare session where patient health data and/or PGHD like heart rate or steps, is collected or reviewed, connecting patients, practitioners, and organizations to show their relationship with the data.
PGHDObservation

Stores health data from patients, like heart rate, steps, or blood pressure with also contextual information like posture or location which may helpful in making clinical decisions.
PGHDOrganization

Information about an institution or health facility like a hospital or clinic, that handles patient generated health data and/or health data to track who is involved. Describes an organization, such as a hospital or telehealth service, responsible for collecting or managing patient health data.
PGHDPatient

Details about a patient who shares their Patient Generated Health Data, like sleep, heart rate or steps, to link it to their medical records for doctors to use. Represents a patient who generates health data, such as activity or sleep duration, with required details like name and ID to ensure accurate data linkage in clinical systems.
PGHDPractitioner

Details about a doctor or health worker who checks or uses patient health data and/or patient generated health data (PGHD). Represents a healthcare practitioner who validates or works with patient health data, with required details like name and ID.
PGHDProvenance

Shows where patient health data came from, what device was used, practitioner, and organization involved.

Structures: Extension Definitions

These define constraints on FHIR data types for systems conforming to this implementation guide.

ObservationProvenance

An extension for observation that links it to provenance data showing where it came from and agents involved.
PGHDLocation

Records where the patient was, like home or outside, when health data was collected.
PGHDPosture

Posture of the patient during observation, like sitting or standing, when health data was collected.

Terminology: Value Sets

These define sets of codes used by systems conforming to this implementation guide.

Encounter Class ValueSet

A list of encounter classes including Ambulatory and Home Health.
PGHD Body Site

A list of allowed body sites where the PGHD was collected from or device was placed.
PGHD Location Values

Common patient locations during PGHD collection.
PGHD Posture Values

Common patient postures during PGHD collection.
