Resource Profile: PGHDEncounter

Official URL: https://w3id.org/pghdprovo/fhir/StructureDefinition/pghd-encounter Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-09-21 Computable Name: PGHDEncounter

Records a session or visit where patient health data and/or patient generated health data (PGHD) is collected or reviewed. Captures details of a healthcare session where patient health data and/or PGHD like heart rate or steps, is collected or reviewed, connecting patients, practitioners, and organizations to show their relationship with the data.

Usages:

  • Refer to this Profile: PGHDCommunication and PGHDObservation
  • Examples for this Profile: Encounter/EncounterExample
